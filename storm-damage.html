Storm Damage & Insurance

Restoring Your Home After Severe Weather

Understanding Hail Damage

Hail can cause significant damage to your roof that isn't always visible from the ground. It can bruise shingles, causing them to lose granules and eventually fail, leading to leaks. Wind can lift shingles and break the sealant/bond. It is crucial to get a professional inspection after a major storm.

Tip: Most insurance policies have a limited time window to file a claim. Don't wait!

The 7-Step Insurance Claim Process

1

Inspection

We inspect your roof for hail or wind damage.

2

File Claim

You contact your insurance company to file a claim.

3

Adjustment

An adjuster visits to assess the damage scope.

4

Approval

Insurance approves the claim and sends a summary.

5

Material Selection

We help you choose the best materials for replacement.

6

Installation

Our team installs your new roof professionally.

7

Completion

We submit final invoices to insurance for depreciation release.
