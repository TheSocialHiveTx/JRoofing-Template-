Our Story

Family Owned. Locally Operated. 18+ Years of Excellence.

From Subcontractor to Prime Contractor

ZMAC Roofing (ZMAC Construction) began its journey over 18 years ago, initially operating as a trusted subcontractor for large regional roofing companies. We built our reputation on the roof, learning every detail of commercial and residential systems.

In 2017/2018, we expanded to become a direct prime contractor, bringing our deep field experience directly to homeowners and business owners. This transition allowed us to control quality, pricing, and the customer experience from start to finish.

Why Choose ZMAC?

In-House Labor

We rely on our own skilled workforce, ensuring consistent quality and accountability on every project.

Purchasing Power

Our volume of large commercial projects allows us to negotiate better material rates, passing the savings to you.

Transparency

We believe in honest assessments and clear communication. No hidden fees or surprise costs.
